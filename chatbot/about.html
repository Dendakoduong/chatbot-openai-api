About

What is BMO?

BMO is Finn and Jake's living video game console system, portable electrical outlet, computer, and friend. BMO is characterized as a loyal, trusting and helpful robot who is protective of Finn and Jake.
BMO looks like a Macintosh computer with Game Boy Color buttons and Atari 2600 controllers. He also shares some traits with a Vectrex.
BMO is a medium-sized teal/blue-green gaming device, sometimes appearing with one or two controllers attached to them. When not being played, they have an 8-bit face.

What is BAT?

BAT (BMO Assistant Transformer) is a chatbot same name with BMO, using OpenAI API. You can ask BAT questions, start a conversation, use it to write short stories, and more.
